The Story These Charts Tell

  • High-tech is the rising star — ~5% of GDP from startups alone; 150K jobs; $51.9B exports growing 13.4% YoY; 6 unicorns
  • Automotive still dominates traditional industry — 4.3% of GDP directly, ~10% with supply chain; 434K jobs; but increasingly threatened by EV transition
  • Mining is declining but significant — ~1% of GDP, 18K jobs; 3rd largest EU coal producer; 4th globally in kaolin; 2033 coal phase-out looms
  • Beer punches above its weight — Small GDP share (~0.5%) but 550 breweries and huge cultural significance
  • Glass is niche but prestigious — 7,000 jobs, $2.4B exports, 750+ years of tradition
  • Agriculture is shrinking — Down to 1.6% of GDP, but Czechia remains cereal self-sufficient
