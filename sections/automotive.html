The Engine

Automotive: The Dominant Industry 4.3% of GDP

In 2024, Czech factories produced 1,452,881 vehicles—a new record (+3.9%). Vehicle manufacturing alone contributes €12.4B / 4.3% of GDP (Eurostat)—highest in the EU. With the full supply chain, the IMF estimates ~10% of GDP and ~434,000 jobs (~8% of workforce). EVs: 10.4% of production (151,162 units).

Where Do Czech-Made Škodas Go? (2024)

Germany alone takes 187,100 Škodas—more than double what stays in Czechia. Škoda 2024 total: 926,600 vehicles globally (+6.9%).
