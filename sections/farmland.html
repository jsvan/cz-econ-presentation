Beyond Hops

What Covers Czech Farmland? 1.6% of GDP

Czechia has 3.5 million hectares of agricultural land (~45% of country). Of this, 71% is arable, 27% permanent grassland, and just 1% is hops/vineyards/orchards. Hops occupy only 0.14% of farmland but are culturally iconic. Czechia is self-sufficient in cereals; ~1/3 is exported.

Agricultural Context

  • Self-sufficient in cereals; ~1/3 exported
  • Agriculture = 1.6% of GDP (2023)
  • ~2.5% of workforce in agriculture
  • 50% of farmland in "less favorable" areas (LFA)
  • Plant production dominates over animal production
