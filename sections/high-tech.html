Why Is Czech High-Tech Outperforming?

  • Central EU location: Same-day logistics to Germany, Austria, Poland
  • Cost arbitrage: Software engineers earn ~40% less than in Germany despite proximity
  • Engineering tradition: Strong technical universities, industrial heritage
  • Foxconn effect: $7B electronics giant trains workforce, attracts supply chain
  • Brain gain: Reverse migration as Czechs return from Silicon Valley with experience

Challenge: The IMF warns that "higher value-added sectors, including ICT services, are constrained by lack of skilled labor and limited access to capital."
