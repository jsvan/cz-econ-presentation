Craft Heritage

Bohemian Crystal: 750+ Years ~0.7% of GDP*

Czech glassmaking dates to the 13th century (~1250 AD). After WWI, Czechoslovakia inherited 90% of Austria-Hungary's glass industry. Today over 90% is exported to 90+ countries—$2.4 billion in 2023. The industry employs 7,000+ people across ~2,000 enterprises.

Major Companies

  • Preciosa — Crystal chandeliers, jewelry. 30% lead content for sparkle.
  • Moser (Karlovy Vary) — Luxury crystal, 160+ years old, manual manufacture.
  • Crystalex — "Bohemia Crystal" trademark.
  • Lasvit — Modern design firm, custom installations worldwide.
  • Rückl — Traditional Bohemian glass since 1846.

Historic Significance — Wikipedia

  • 18th century: eclipsed Venetian glass
  • Chandeliers in palaces of Louis XV, Maria Theresa, Elizabeth of Russia
  • Glass school in Železný Brod (founded 1920)
  • Industry concentrated in northern Bohemia
  • Some techniques preserved by only a handful of masters
